Thunderstorm

What is a Thunderstorm?

A thunderstorm is a storm with lightning and thunder. Its produced by a cumulonimbus cloud, usually producing gusty winds, heavy rain and sometimes hail.

What causes Thunderstorm and where do they happen?

For a thunderstorm to form the following conditions must be present: moisture, unstable air that must be relatively warm and rising quickly. Finally, there must be a updraft. It can be formed by fronts, sea breezes or mountains.

What is the biggest Thunderstorm in the world history?



Throughout human history, there were many huge and dangerous thunderstorms, but the biggest one recorded was in India, and it occurred on December 1st, 2014. It was the highest voltage thunderstorm ever recorded, with 1.3 billion volts.
